Forlorn Four Instructions (hastily written at 12:30 AM)

Your goal is to find the valuable artifact (symbolized by a '*') on the 10th level of the dungeon.

You create a party of four adventurers, selected from seven classes:

  • The fighter is an all-around competent adventurer.
  • The berserker attacks well, but is damaged more easily.
  • The stalwart is a good defender, but has a weak attack.
  • The rogue is weaker than the figher, but may not be noticed by monsters.
  • The assassin has a very strong attack and also may sneak past monsters, but is very weak defending.
  • The mage has a strong magic blast, but is weak physically.
  • The cleric can heal allies, but is also weak physically.

Controls:

  • numpad or hjklyubn — move (or attack, if the square is occupied by a monster)
  • > and < — descend or ascend stairs (represented by > and < symbols)
  • . — end turn early
  • c — cast a spell; move the cursor with the movement keys and hit space to select a square
  • d — describe a monster; move the cursor with the movement keys and hit space to select a monster
